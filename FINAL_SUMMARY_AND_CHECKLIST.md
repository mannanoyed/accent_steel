## KEY MESSAGES TO REPEAT

Throughout the meeting, come back to these key points:

1. **"This solves a real, current problem."**
   Not hypothetical. They're losing money now.

2. **"The system lives in your existing tools."**
   Email, Slack, Stromis. Not a new platform.

3. **"Everything is automated."**
   No manual forwarding. No separate logging. No busywork.

4. **"Every decision is auditable."**
   Timestamped proof of causation. Critical for cost recovery.

5. **"This pays for itself in 4-6 weeks."**
   Not a long-term play. Fast ROI.

6. **"We're not asking you to change how you work."**
   We're enhancing your existing workflow.

## RED FLAGS IF YOU SEE THEM

**If they say "We don't really have a revision problem":**
└─ Gently ask: "How many revisions are you getting per month?" Usually reveals they have more than they realize.

**If they say "Our Stromis integration is too complex":**
└─ Respond: "We've handled complex integrations before. Let's do a technical deep dive with your IT team."

**If they seem focused only on cost:**
└─ Acknowledge: "Yes, the ROI is there. But also, you'll have visibility you don't have now. That's valuable too."

**If they seem concerned about disruption:**
└─ Reassure: "Phase 1 is purely additive. We're not changing your current process. We're adding intelligence on top of it."
